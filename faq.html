Our Story

Americas Marketing Company Limited

Americas Marketing Company Limited was established in 2001, with its headquarters located at #22 Ramjohn Trace, Penal, Trinidad and Tobago. What began as a hardware distribution company has grown into the AMCOL Group of Companies - a diversified organisation serving customers across multiple industries.

Today, the AMCOL Group comprises six businesses: AMCOL Hardware & Home Store, AMCOL Industrial, AMCOL Haulage, AMCOL Fitness First Health Club, AMCOL Supplements Store and AMCOL Real Estate.

With a strong customer base throughout Trinidad and Tobago and the wider Caribbean, AMCOL serves residential, commercial and industrial clients with reliable products, professional services and customer-focused solutions.

The AMCOL Group has established itself as one of the nation's leading distributors and service providers, operating across hardware and building supplies, industrial equipment, transportation and haulage, health and fitness, real estate, and the oil, gas and petrochemical sectors.

Driven by quality, reliability and continuous growth, AMCOL remains committed to delivering trusted brands, dependable service and lasting value to every customer and business partner.

How to Order

Ordering from AMCOL Hardware

  • Browse the product catalogue and open the product details page for the item you need.
  • Select Add to cart to save products for your order request.
  • Review your cart, adjust quantities, and check the estimated total before checkout.
  • Choose either pickup at the Penal store or delivery where available.
  • Fill out your name, phone number, preferred date and time, plus delivery address if delivery is selected.
  • Submit the order request. AMCOL will confirm product availability, final pricing, and any delivery fee by phone.
How do I check if a hardware item is available?

Use the product catalogue to search by product name, brand, department, category, model, or product ID. Availability can change, so AMCOL will confirm stock after you submit an order request or contact the store.

Can I order tools, plumbing, electrical, paint, and building materials online?

Yes. Add catalogue products to your cart and submit the request for pickup or delivery. The team will confirm item availability, quantities, and final pricing before the order is prepared.

Do prices shown online include the final total?

The cart gives an estimated product total based on catalogue pricing. AMCOL will confirm final pricing, substitutions, and delivery charges by phone before the order is completed.

Can I pick up my order at the Penal store?

Yes. Choose pickup during checkout, enter your preferred pickup date and time, and AMCOL will confirm when your items are ready at 22 Ramjohn Trace, Penal.

Does AMCOL offer delivery?

Delivery may be available depending on location, item size, and order details. Choose delivery during checkout and include your full address. Delivery fees are confirmed by phone.

What information should I include for a material quote?

Include the product name, product ID if available, quantity, preferred brand or size, delivery area if needed, and any project requirements such as lumber lengths, pipe size, paint finish, or electrical rating.

Can business or contractor customers place bulk orders?

Yes. Contractors, maintenance teams, and business customers can contact AMCOL for bulk sourcing, industrial supply support, and product availability checks.

Who should I call if I need help choosing the right product?

Call the main counter at 647-7653 or the sales office at +1 (868) 288-5800. The team can help with item selection and availability.
