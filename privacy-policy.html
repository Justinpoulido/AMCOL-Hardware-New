Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, when it may be shared, and the choices available to you.

This Privacy Policy applies to the website at www.amcolhardwarett.com and to AMCOL Hardware, Americas Marketing Company Limited, 22 Ramjohn Trace, Penal, Trinidad and Tobago.

By using this website or submitting information through it, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We May Collect

The information we collect depends on how you use the website.

Information you provide

You may provide personal information when you:

  • Submit an online inquiry
  • Request information about a product
  • Request a quotation
  • Create or manage a customer account
  • Add products to your cart
  • Submit an order, pickup or delivery request
  • Contact us by telephone or email
  • Communicate with our customer service or sales team

This information may include:

  • Full name, email address and telephone number
  • Account login information
  • Billing, pickup or delivery address
  • Products, quantities and brands requested
  • Preferred pickup or delivery details
  • Information included in your inquiry or message
  • Business or company information
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide
Please do not submit confidential or sensitive personal information unless it is necessary for your request.

Information collected automatically

When a visitor uses the website, certain technical information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Internet Protocol address
  • Browser type, device type and operating system
  • Referring website
  • Pages viewed
  • Date and time of visits
  • General website interaction and usage information
  • Cookie and session information

This information helps AMCOL understand website usage, maintain security and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

AMCOL Hardware may use collected information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and customer requests
  • Check product availability and prepare quotations
  • Process and review order requests
  • Arrange pickup or delivery
  • Contact customers regarding an inquiry, quotation or order
  • Create and manage customer accounts
  • Maintain products placed in a customer's cart
  • Provide customer service
  • Improve website content, navigation and performance
  • Diagnose technical problems
  • Prevent fraud, misuse and unauthorized access
  • Maintain business, accounting and transaction records
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements
Submitting an inquiry or order request does not automatically confirm product availability, final pricing or acceptance of an order. AMCOL Hardware may contact you to verify those details.

3. Customer Accounts

Customers may be able to create an account to manage personal details, saved products, carts or order requests.

Users are responsible for:

  • Providing accurate information
  • Keeping login details confidential
  • Protecting access to their device and account
  • Informing AMCOL if they believe their account has been accessed without authorization

AMCOL may suspend or restrict an account where fraud, misuse or a security risk is reasonably suspected.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website may use cookies and similar technologies to keep the website functioning properly, remember user preferences, maintain login sessions, preserve cart information, understand website usage, and improve performance and security.

A cookie is a small data file stored on a visitor's device by their browser. Users may block or delete cookies, but some website features may not function correctly. Where legally required, visitors may be given a choice regarding optional cookies.

5. Google Maps and Embedded Content

The Contact page may include an embedded Google Maps preview. When users interact with the map, Google may collect device information, browser information, IP address, map interaction information, cookies or similar tracking information.

Embedded third-party content may behave similarly to visiting the third party's website directly. AMCOL Hardware does not control how Google or other third parties process information through their services.

6. Analytics

Analytics tools may be used to understand which pages are visited, how visitors navigate the website, which devices and browsers are used, and general traffic and performance trends.

Where possible, this information is reviewed in aggregated form.

7. How We Share Information

AMCOL Hardware does not sell or rent personal information to advertisers.

Information may be shared only when reasonably necessary with service providers that assist with:

  • Website hosting and database storage
  • Customer account management
  • Email delivery
  • Website security and technical support
  • Order fulfilment, pickup or delivery services
  • Payment processing, where applicable
  • Analytics and performance monitoring

Information may also be disclosed with customer consent, to complete a requested service, to comply with a legal obligation, in response to a lawful request from an authority, to protect the rights, safety or property of AMCOL Hardware, its customers or others, or in connection with a business restructuring, merger or transfer.

8. External Links

The website may contain links to Google Maps, manufacturer websites, supplier websites, social media platforms and external service providers.

AMCOL does not control those websites and is not responsible for their content, security or privacy practices.

9. Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical and organisational safeguards may be used to protect personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, alteration, disclosure and destruction.

No method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. AMCOL Hardware cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Data Retention

Personal information is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to respond to requests, manage customer accounts, complete orders or quotations, maintain business and transaction records, resolve disputes, prevent fraud, and meet legal, accounting or regulatory obligations.

Retention periods may vary based on the type of information and why it was collected.

11. Access, Correction and Deletion

Customers may contact AMCOL Hardware to request access to personal information, correction of inaccurate information, updating of incomplete information, deletion where legally and operationally permitted, restriction of certain uses, or withdrawal of marketing consent.

Some information may still need to be retained for completed transactions, accounting records, legal obligations, fraud prevention, security investigations, dispute resolution, or protection of another person's rights.

12. Account Deletion

To request deletion of your customer account, contact AMCOL Hardware using the details provided in the Contact Us section of this Privacy Policy.

Account deletion may be permanent and may remove access to saved account information. Certain transaction, legal, security or accounting records may be retained even after the account itself is deleted.

13. Children's Privacy

The website is intended for general retail and business customers and is not specifically directed to children. AMCOL does not knowingly seek personal information from children without appropriate authorization.

14. Marketing Communications

Customers who choose to receive marketing communications may withdraw consent by using an unsubscribe option, contacting AMCOL Hardware, or updating communication preferences where available.

Service-related messages about active inquiries, quotations, accounts or orders may still be sent where necessary.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated due to website changes, new services, business-process changes, security improvements, or legal and regulatory requirements.

The latest version will be posted with an updated revision date.
